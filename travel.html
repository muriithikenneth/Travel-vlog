Travel blogs

Travel blogs can be fantastic resources for inspiration,tips and insights into different destinations

Here are some of the examples:

    • Nomadic Matt
    • The blonde abroad
    • Expert vagabond
    • A broken backpack

  1. Nomadic matt

    2. Nomadic mats site,led by the well traveled Matt Kepnes,breaks the myth that travel has to be expensive

    He shares budget tips,destination guides and an advice for travelling sustainability

  2. The blonde abroad

    3. Kiesten rich focuses on solo female travel,offering advice on destinations,packaging and photography

    Kiki’s got you covered with awesome tools for booking flights, tips on travel cards, gear suggestions, and even job opportunities for travelers.

  3. Expert vagabond

    4. Expert Vagabond is a super versatile travel blog great for all types of travelers.

    It attracts a wide audience through social media and search engines, showcasing its broad appeal.

  4. A broken backpack

    5. Melissa, a Canadian traveler since 2014, runs this cool blog showing you don’t need a pile of cash to see the world.

    She’s all about budget-friendly travel and even shares how to earn money while you’re out exploring.
